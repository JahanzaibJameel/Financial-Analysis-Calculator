# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all individuals with respect and consideration
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome diverse perspectives and contributions
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together to solve problems and build relationships
- **Be Constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be Professional**: Maintain professional conduct in all interactions

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment**: Unwelcome comments, advances, or imagery
- **Discrimination**: Any form of discrimination or exclusion
- **Disrespect**: Disruptive, disrespectful, or unprofessional behavior
- **Privacy**: Publishing private information without permission
- **Spam**: Unsolicited commercial content or self-promotion

## Reporting

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact:

- **Email**: conduct@financial-analysis-calculator.com
- **GitHub Issues**: [Create a private issue](https://github.com/your-username/financial-analysis-calculator/issues/new)
- **Project Maintainers**: Direct message to any project maintainer

### What to Include

- **Description**: Clear description of the incident
- **Parties Involved**: Who was affected
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, logs, or other documentation
- **Time**: When the incident occurred

## Enforcement

### Process

1. **Review**: All reports will be reviewed by project maintainers
2. **Investigation**: We will investigate the situation thoroughly
3. **Action**: Appropriate action will be taken based on findings
4. **Follow-up**: We will follow up with all parties involved

### Possible Actions

- **Warning**: Private warning for minor infractions
- **Temporary Ban**: Temporary suspension for repeated issues
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal for severe violations
- **Legal Action**: Involvement of law enforcement if necessary
